Brice is Rice

There once was a small peasant whos name was Brice, he lived under a bridge with bags of rice.

A young boy walked up to him, whose name was Creed, in which he asks Brice, "May I taketh some Riceth?"

Brice then said no and banished the young boy away.

Brice the peasant with rice blistfully walks into the sunset unaware of what comes next.
